Lexique 4
=========

Lexique 4.00 provides frequency measures from a 316M-word subtitle corpus,
 contextual diversity, phonological representations, lemmas, morphological
  structure, prevalence, and lexical decision data for 190,000 French word forms.
  
**Table:**
[Lexique4.zip](http://www.lexique.org/databases/Lexique4/Lexique4.zip)
